On Zara.com you can complete your purchase either as a GUEST or a REGISTERED customer. Registering is very easy. We will only ask you for the information we need to speed up the purchase process. You will find format examples in the registration form to help you when you enter details such as your telephone number. Your order information will always be available to you through the link your account if you are a registered client or through the link we send you in your confirmation email if you are a guest customer. What’s more, now you’ve got your ZARA QR. You can find it by logging on to the Zara APP, registering as a customer and going to the “Purchases” section. You can link all your purchases to this ZARA QR, both online and from physical stores.
